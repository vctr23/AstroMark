# **App Name**: AstroMark

## Core Features:

- User & Business Profile Setup: Securely register and identify business owners via URL, email, or full name, allowing for initial business profile configuration.
- Market Gap & Competitor Analysis Tool: An AI-powered tool to analyze the user's business against the market, identifying competitive landscape and potential missing opportunities.
- AI Customer Persona & Targeting: An AI tool that assists in defining customer types and suggesting optimal sectors and locations for marketing campaigns based on business data.
- AI Marketing Content Generation: Generate marketing ideas, catchy slogans, and visual banners using an AI tool based on the defined business and customer persona.
- Campaign ROI Calculator: Calculate the projected Return on Investment for marketing campaigns by inputting costs and expected gains.
- Basic Campaign Tracking Dashboard: A simplified dashboard to monitor key metrics for active marketing campaigns and their performance.
- Data Export & AI API Integration: Enable exporting of campaign data in JSON, CSV, and TXT formats. Provide basic configuration for connecting to user-selected external AI APIs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark scheme to evoke a 'futuristic' and 'espacial' feel. The primary color is a deep, electric violet (#681FFF), symbolizing creativity and future technology. The background is a desaturated, very dark purple-grey (#17141A) to provide depth. The accent color is a vibrant yet cool blue (#6280FF) for highlights and interactive elements, analogous to the primary to maintain harmony.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its techy, futuristic appeal. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean readability and modern aesthetics.
- Use sleek, minimalist line-art icons with a subtle glow effect, reflecting a futuristic, space-themed interface.
- Adopt a clean, structured layout with asymmetric elements to add a dynamic and futuristic feel. Prioritize clear information hierarchy, dark panels, and possibly transparent overlays, hinting at advanced displays.
- Implement smooth transitions between sections and subtle hover effects on interactive elements. Consider gentle particle effects or slow-pulsing glows to enhance the 'espacial' and futuristic user experience.
